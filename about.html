About Football Legends

Welcome to Football Legends, a fan‑made website dedicated to celebrating the greatest football clubs in history. Here you'll find information about legendary clubs, their iconic players, and their trophy cabinets.

This site was created as a learning project to practice web development with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and GitHub Pages. It showcases five of the most successful and beloved clubs in world football: Real Madrid, AC Milan, Manchester United, Bayern Munich, and Barcelona.

Feel free to explore each club's page and learn about their rich histories. If you have any feedback or suggestions, visit the Contact page – I'd love to hear from you!
