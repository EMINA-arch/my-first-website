Real Madrid CF

  • Founded: 1902
  • Stadium: Santiago Bernabéu (81,044)
  • League: La Liga
  • European Cups: 14
  • Nickname: Los Blancos

History

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a Spanish professional football club based in Madrid. Founded in 1902, it is one of the most successful and widely supported clubs in the world. The club is known for its all‑white kit and its fierce rivalry with Barcelona, known as El Clásico.

The club's greatest era came in the 1950s when they won five consecutive European Cups, led by Alfredo Di Stéfano. In the modern era, they have added more European titles, including the famous "La Décima" in 2014 and three consecutive titles from 2016 to 2018.
