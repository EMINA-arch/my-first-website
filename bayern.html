Bayern Munich

  • Founded: 1900
  • Stadium: Allianz Arena (75,000)
  • League: Bundesliga
  • European Cups: 6
  • Nickname: Die Bayern

History

Fußball‑Club Bayern München, commonly known as Bayern Munich, is a German professional sports club based in Munich. Founded in 1900, it is the most successful club in German football history, having won a record 32 Bundesliga titles and 6 European Cups. The club's golden era included the 1970s with Franz Beckenbauer and Gerd Müller, and more recently the treble‑winning seasons under Jupp Heynckes (2013) and Hansi Flick (2020).
